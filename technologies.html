Systems & Methods

3D Printing Technologies

Compare mechanical tolerances, laser sinter mechanisms, and extrusion specifications across our fleet of production printers.

Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM)

Fused Deposition Modeling extrudes thermal polymers (PLA, ABS, PETG) layer-by-layer through a heated nozzle. Ideal for rapid validation fit tests, brackets, housings, and low-stress mechanical parts.

Layer Height 100 - 300 microns
Dimensional Tolerance ±0.5% (min ±0.5mm)
Surface Finish Textured layer lines

Stereolithography (SLA)

Stereolithography cures liquid photosensitive resin with an ultraviolet laser point-by-point. Best suited for ultra-fine cosmetic details, dental guides, micro-fluidic assemblies, and jewelry modeling patterns.

Layer Height 25 - 100 microns
Dimensional Tolerance ±0.1% (min ±0.1mm)
Surface Finish Extremely smooth / glass-like
