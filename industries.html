Vertical Markets

Industries Served

Providing high-tolerance component batch printing across aerospace systems, medical devices, automotive engineering, and consumer goods markets.

Aerospace & Defense

Custom lightweight manifolds, engine nozzles, brackets, and structural metal components sintered in titanium alloys. Meets extreme thermal and pressure constraints.

Medical & Dental

Patient-matched orthopedic joints, dental surgical templates, and clear anatomical visual guides printed using biocompatible and sterilizable resins.

Automotive & Racing

Functional dashboard prototype assemblies, gear shifters, air intake ducts, and high-temp glass-filled polymer brackets engineered under strict timelines.

Consumer & Industrial

Custom footwear midsoles, headphone enclosures, architectural mockups, and low-volume enclosures batch-sintered with durable Nylon PA12 polymers.
