Our Vision

Decentralizing Additive Manufacturing

Stackly 3D was founded with a singular purpose: to link high-precision industrial additive printers with global development pipelines via slick, browser-native CAD submission suites.

Who We Are

Empowering Engineers & Innovators Globally

Stackly 3D simplifies industrial additive manufacturing. We bridge the gap between complex CAD designs and physical high-precision parts by offering an enterprise-grade cloud fabrication platform.

Our platform automates geometric mesh checkouts, provides immediate wall-thickness analysis, and dispatches manufacturing assets directly to audited local and international production nodes.

Cloud Slicing Engines Automated STL mesh analysis and solid wall thickness repairs in the browser.
Global Sintering Farm Direct access to metal sintering (Titanium) and SLS Nylon printers globally.
