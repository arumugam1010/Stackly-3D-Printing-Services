Our Vision

Decentralizing Additive Manufacturing

Stackly 3D was founded with a singular purpose: to link high-precision industrial additive printers with global development pipelines via slick, browser-native CAD submission suites.

Our Journey

Milestones & History

2026 - Global Additive Cloud

Fitted smart APIs linking customers, automated validator checkers, and CNC operations into a single continuous delivery manufacturing dashboard.

2024 - DMLS Metal Printing Launch

Added metal sintering (Grade 5 Titanium & Aerospace Aluminum alloys) to support stress-critical aerospace and prosthetic print actions.

2022 - High-Speed SLS Slicers

Constructed automated polygon-slicing clusters to verify mesh wall thicknesses in real-time, eliminating physical print failure overheads.

2020 - Garage Origins

Created with two FDM printers and a custom CAD rendering client. Integrated immediate prototyping feedback tools for engineers.
