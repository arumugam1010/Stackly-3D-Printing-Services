Design Rules

Additive Design Constraints

Minimum Wall Thickness

To avoid warping or fracture, observe our structural guidelines. SLA resin: 0.5mm. FDM polymers: 0.8mm. Metal DMLS: 1.0mm.

Wall Limit: 0.5mm - 1.0mm

Overhang Thresholds

Self-supporting limits require no scaffolding. Design features with angles over 45° relative to the build plate to print support-free.

Scaffold-Free Limit: 45°

Interlocking Clearance

To ensure moving hinges, joints, and gears slice cleanly and do not fuse, preserve a minimum gap tolerance of 0.3mm in CAD files.

Clearance Limit: 0.3mm
